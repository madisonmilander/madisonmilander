Process_Step:
Process_Description:
Created blank Excel spreadsheet, began by initially populating with OSP codes and other selected attribute data from the RIGIS ritown5k dataset. Added new Name, Address, Municipality, ZIP, community type, government model, year established, year incorporated, and website fields. Copied address and other attribute data from <http://www.ri.gov/towns>. Updated incomplete address data for Little Compton, Warren, Westerly. Updated URL's for Coventry, North Providence. Geocoded against RIE911 address location data. Compared locations of city and town halls against prior version of dataset distributed by RIGIS via the "near" ArcGIS command. Reviewed and confirmed significant location differences between the datasets using supplemental information from municipal websites, RIE911 sites data, and Google Street View (when available).
